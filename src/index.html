This project archives the Tweets of former President Donald J. Trump. On January 8th, 2021, the President was permanently banned from Twitter.com, after inciting an attack on the United States Capitol.

The archive functions as a drop-in replacement for the now removed tweets: https://twitter.com/realDonaldTrump/status/98454970654916600 --> https://trumptwitter.org/realDonaldTrump/status/98454970654916600
